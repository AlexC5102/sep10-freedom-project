Overview

Technology has changed the way businesses work by making things faster, easier, and more connected. Tools like computers, apps, and websites help people communicate, share information, and manage money. Apps like Slack, PayPal, and Zoom are great examples—they let teams chat, send payments, and hold meetings online. In the future, new inventions like smart tables, AI chatbots, and language-translating devices could make business even more advanced and helpful.

PayPal is a fast, secure way to pay online. We help you make purchases at millions of online stores across more than 200 global markets – all without the hassle of converting currency first.
